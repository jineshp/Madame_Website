meet the stylist

Mitali Ambekar

Mitali Ambekar is a freelance stylist who has been a part of the fashion industry for the last five years. In this short span of time she has worked on several television commercials, fashion shoots, print campaigns, movie shoots and fashion shows.

Mitali is an alumni of the renowned Istituto Marangoni, Milan. Her style is very conceptual and experimental.

She has worked with celebrities like Prachi Desai, Mugdha Godse, Muzammil Ibrahim, Richa Chadda, Monica Bedi, Sonalee Kulkarni to name a few. She has also been associated with fashion and lifestyle brands like: Wella Hair Professionals, Marriott Hotel, Oster India, Femina Magazine, Krome Furnitures, A la Mode, Rocky S Designs and many more.
