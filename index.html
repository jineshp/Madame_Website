About MSW

As we say "Fashion changes but style endures."

Designers have always been the face of any major Fashion events taking place around the globe.Where as this time we bringing the stylists in front. Our vision is to get the stylist into the limelight, which has never been done before.A stylist usually being at a creative position is always heard of as the behind the stage magician. One who is responsible for the entire image and outlook of their client, the ones who are qualified to convert a popper into a princess.Hence Style week is all about the visual communication of a stylist. The idea is to push boundaries and show creativity as well as cultivate a literary style. So as toInterpret the soul of an article of clothing and communicate it through a unique style, through the fashion sense of a Stylist.A Fashion Stylist is distinct from an Image Consultant, hence the sole limelight of the show are the stylists. Style week has selected the top best 5 stylists of India in order to visually communicate through their style.An asset of a fashion stylist is the ability to take what's new and make it look eye-catching and attractive, so you'll be inspired to try it yourself. Hence creating a benchmark for all the Stylists in the show.

The show will include the elaboration of a particular style through the construction of a total look and capture it in order to trademark the unique style of a particular stylist. By enhancing the collections of designers, informing and inspiring others. The stylists in this show will define the winning combination of component parts for an image that is needed to emphasize the style of a brand, a magazine or a person. Fashion stylists fill roles that are pivotal to obtaining the success of a collection. Hence the stylists will portray the interpretation of the soul article of clothing and communicate it through a unique style.
